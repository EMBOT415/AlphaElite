Daniel

trainer

Since a young adult, I have put myself through a trial and error process to figure out how to master my own personal health and fitness. Through perseverance and hard work, I believe that I have found the secrets behind what it takes to achieve the best body you can have. I now want to inspire you to overcome any mental barriers or obstacles that you may have. Since a young age, I competed in national wrestling and judo tournaments, as well as varsity swimming and basketball. I also spent one year training in the BUDs/SWCC Navy SEAL physical preparatory program. As a well diverse athlete, I look for ways to not only physically challenge myself, but to also help motivate my clients to push past their perceived limited abilities. I currently devote my time to training a wide variety of clients. I also work as a model for Wilhelmina Model Agency. In addition, I attend school for physical therapy and take part in a variety of clinical and private physical therapy internships.

Nick

trainer

My name is Nicholas Daperis and I am a certified physical trainer. Since a very young age, I have naturally and instinctively been interested in physical activity, whether it is sports or martial arts. I began gymnastics at a young age and hold a second-degree black belt in Tae Kwon Do. I played basketball on championship teams and Varsity in high school. I have also participated in the Daily News Golden Gloves Boxing Tournament and I am currently training to compete this year as well. I am passionate, dedicated and focused with all clients, from young to mature adults. Physical Training is the springboard to be the best one can be mentally, physically, and emotionally. Total well-being is a top priority in my program. Proper diet and an exercise routine is the key to success. All ages are accommodated to reach their full potential and goal. I currently work as an independent trainer on West 28th Street and 5th Avenue in Manhattan. I also train privately at residences. There is also the opportunity to purchase individual on-line programs for a one-time payment. I have a vested interest in my clients individual needs and goals with a personally tailored program resulting in complete satisfaction.
